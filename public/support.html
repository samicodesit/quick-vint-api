Support & Help Center

Find answers to common questions below. If you can't find what you're looking for, we're here to help.

How do I install AutoLister AI?

AutoLister AI is a Chrome Extension. You can install it in seconds from the Chrome Web Store. Just click the "Get Extension" button on our homepage or in the navigation bar. After adding it to Chrome, we recommend pinning it to your toolbar for easy access!

How do I use the extension on Vinted?

Once installed, navigate to the listing creation page on Vinted. As you upload your photos, you'll see the Generate button. Click it, and our AI will instantly generate a compelling title and description for you to review and use.

The AutoLister icon isn't appearing. What should I do?

First, try refreshing the Vinted page. If that doesn't work, ensure the extension is enabled in your Chrome browser's extension manager (you can type chrome://extensions in your address bar). If you still have trouble, please reach out to our support team.

How do I manage my subscription or change my plan?

You can view all our available plans on the Pricing page. To manage an active subscription, click on the AutoLister AI extension icon in your browser toolbar and click Manage Subscription.

Is AutoLister AI officially affiliated with Vinted?

No, AutoLister AI is an independent, third-party tool created to help sellers be more successful and efficient. We are not officially affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to Vinted in any way.
